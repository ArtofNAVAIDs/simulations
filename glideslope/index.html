Localizer Simulation

This simulation demonstrates how localizer power, modulation, and phase effect the localizer signal. Knobs and Pots are based on the Mark 1 system. The blue rotary knobs do not function do to flash conversion errors but SBO can be adusted by the "SBO Pwr" pot. SBO phase can be toggled using the radio buttons to understand the concepts.
